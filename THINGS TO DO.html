FUNWORLD

Funworld Durban is a beachfront amusement park set along OR Tambo Parade in Durban ,KwaZulu-Natal.Built in the 1970's ,the fun park is set along the Golden Mile ,a 5km lenght of sandy swimming beaches .The park provides safe and secure entertainment for the whole family with a variety of rides for the young and old

USHAKA MARINE WORLD

Ushaka Marine World is one of the most exciting places to visit in the city of Durban ,situated on Durbans Golden Miles and perched just outside the entrance to Africas busiest pote,Ushaka Marine World is everything you have always looked foward to when it comes to fun by the sea

THE PAVILION MALL

The Pavilion is an iconic Super Regional Shopping Centre that is located along the warm shoreline of sunny Durban ,set within the rich cultural landscape of KwaZulu-Natal .The centre was launched in October 1993 and is the oldest first generation shopping mall in KwaZulu-Natal.

DURBAN BOTANICAL GARDANS

The Durban Botanical Gardens are famous for the orignal specimen of a Cycad that is still widely acknowledged as probably the rarest plant in the world, as well as for it's extensive collection of South Africa
